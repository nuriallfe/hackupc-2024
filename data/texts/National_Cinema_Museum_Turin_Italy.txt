The National Museum of Cinema (Italian: Museo Nazionale del Cinema) located in Turin, Italy, is a motion picture museum fitted out inside the Mole Antonelliana tower. It is operated by the Maria Adriana Prolo Foundation, and the core of its collection is the result of the work of the historian and collector Maria Adriana Prolo. It was housed in the Palazzo Chiablese.
In 2008, with 532,196 visitors, it reached the thirteenth place among the most visited Italian museums.
